Mirror Lake Lyceum
(BridgePoint Church Downtown)

One of St. Petersburg’s oldest historic sites, the Lyceum is located in downtown beside Mirror Lake. Built in 1926, It’s of the Mediterranean-revival style. There is a parking lot on site exclusively for the venue. Nearby hotels include Courtyard by Marriott, The Cordova Inn, and the Hollander Hotel.

Address: 737 3rd Ave N, St. Petersburg, FL 33701
